# 2026-04-12: Freeze + V2 + Qwen3-gen Embedding Results

## Baselines

- **Pure AWR (no BC), ActorCriticAugLN**: `awr_aug_debug` — 16.30 ± 3.61 (50 ep)
- **Unaugmented AWR (obs-only)**: 18.38 ± 2.69 (50 ep, Apr 10 journal)
- **BC+AWR on ActorCriticAugLN**: `awr_bc_aug_debug` — 7.46 ± 4.91 (50 ep) — toxic baseline

## Experiment 1: Freeze BC (4 configs, 50K BC-aware steps)

Pretrained from `awr_aug_debug/final.pth`. Freezing protects the obs pathway during BC training. Oracle-mode Gemini + Qwen3-8B embeddings.

Validation is internal training-time validation on last 8 training files. Online return = 50-ep `eval.eval_online` with live Gemini + Qwen.

| Tag | Freeze | AWR | acc_real | acc_zero | acc_shuf | Δ(r-z) | Online return | Δ vs AWR base (16.30) |
|-----|--------|-----|----------|----------|----------|--------|---------------|------------------------|
| freeze_obs_bc       | obs_branch          | off | 0.9790 | 0.5493 | 0.6232 | +0.4297 | 5.54 ± 3.54  | **-10.76** |
| freeze_obs_bcawr    | obs_branch          | on  | 0.7961 | 0.2004 | 0.6800 | +0.5957 | **16.80 ± 3.49** | **+0.50** |
| freeze_all_bc       | obs_and_post_merge  | off | 0.7627 | 0.1805 | 0.2684 | +0.5822 | 8.56 ± 5.23  | -7.74 |
| freeze_all_bcawr    | obs_and_post_merge  | on  | 0.6495 | 0.1119 | 0.3003 | +0.5376 | 14.68 ± 4.18 | -1.62 |

### Hypothesis verdict

- **freeze_obs_bcawr (16.80) matches/beats AWR baseline (16.30)** — freezing the obs pathway while running BC+AWR successfully protects against BC's obs-pathway corruption. This is the first BC+AWR configuration that does not collapse return — previously BC+AWR unfrozen collapsed from 16→7.46 (-8.84).
- **freeze_all_bcawr (14.68) slightly worse than AWR** — restricting BC to only `hidden_fc1 + ln2` still allows obs+post-merge to benefit from gradients, but the hidden-only pathway is too narrow a channel for BC to learn anything useful; still avoids collapse.
- **Both BC-only configs (freeze_obs_bc=5.54, freeze_all_bc=8.56) remain broken** — BC without AWR continues to be toxic even when restricted. Freezing alone cannot save BC-only; the hidden branch itself becomes a corruption vector (hidden branch memorizes oracle→action mapping with acc_real near 0.76–0.98 but oracle data shifts don't transfer online).
- Accuracy shape confirms mechanism: freeze_obs_bcawr has acc_shuf=0.6800 (**higher than acc_zero=0.20 yet close to acc_real=0.80**) — the model is using hidden embeddings informatively (shuffled-vs-zero gap is big) but is not memorizing individual embeddings (real-vs-shuffled gap is small 0.12).

## Experiment 2: V2 Architecture Comparison (2 configs, 100K steps)

`ActorCriticAugV2`: 3-layer obs + 2-layer hidden (zero-init output) + additive merge. Training from scratch (no pretrained load).

| Tag | Config | acc_real | acc_zero | acc_shuf | Δ(r-z) | Online return | LN baseline | Δ V2-LN |
|-----|--------|----------|----------|----------|--------|---------------|-------------|---------|
| v2_awr_aug    | AWR only              | 0.1295 | 0.1171 | 0.1293 | +0.0124 | 16.72 ± 3.39 | 16.30 (LN) | +0.42 |
| v2_awr_bc_aug | AWR + BC (OF=0.05)    | 0.9125 | 0.8007 | 0.8896 | +0.1119 | 4.74 ± 3.35  | 7.46 (LN)  | **-2.72** |

### Hypothesis verdict

- **v2_awr_aug (16.72) matches LN-arch AWR (16.30)** — the V2 architecture change is neutral on pure AWR. Obs pathway depth and additive merge are interchangeable at this scale.
- **v2_awr_bc_aug (4.74) is WORSE than LN BC+AWR (7.46)** — V2's architecture does **not** naturally handle BC better. If anything, the deeper obs branch makes BC corruption more severe. The zero-init hidden branch should have kept things obs-only at init, but the 100K training cycles flooded all branches with BC gradients.
- acc_real=0.9125 with acc_shuf=0.8896 (gap = 0.023) signals **strong memorization**: model relies equally on real and shuffled embeddings — predictions almost independent of which embedding is passed. Classic BC overfitting.

## Experiment 3: Embedding Comparison — Qwen3-gen (PSF + Qwen3-8B generation, pure AWR)

PSF (predict-state-only) prompt variant. Qwen3-8B generates the narrative directly (not embeds); output layer-30 hidden → 4096-dim. Trained pure AWR (no BC) on 117 files of PSF data.

| Condition | NLL | Accuracy |
|-----------|-----|----------|
| Held-out train real | 2.2384 | 42.98% |
| Held-out train zero | 2.2640 | 42.39% |
| Held-out train shuffled | 2.2524 | 42.63% |
| Golden real | 5.6533 | 8.70% |
| Golden zero | 5.6875 | 9.55% |
| Golden shuffled | 5.6443 | 8.93% |

- Δ(zero-real) on train: +0.0256 NLL, -0.6% acc
- Δ(zero-real) on golden: +0.0342 NLL, **+0.85% acc (zero > real!)**

**Online return: 18.62 ± 3.20** vs unaug baseline **18.38 ± 2.69** → **Δ = +0.24**.

### Hypothesis verdict

- Imagination conditioning (Qwen3-gen with PSF) **marginally beats the unaugmented obs-only baseline** on online return. Within noise, but symmetric.
- Validation deltas are tiny (+1.1% NLL, <1% acc). Most of the policy signal comes from obs, which explains the flat validation deltas.
- On golden (human) data, zero even beats real (9.55% vs 8.70% acc). Human states are OOD for the Qwen3-gen embedding distribution.
- Pure AWR benefits very little from imagination at validation time, but the tiny gain materializes in online behavior. Consistent with Apr 8 finding that imagination pathway amplifies BC toxicity but doesn't dominate AWR behavior.
