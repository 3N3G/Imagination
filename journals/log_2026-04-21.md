# 2026-04-21

## Context

Follow-ons to the Apr-19 cadence=5 result (Exp 29: freezenone = 18.58 ± 3.51, content-invariant per Exp 30). Two questions carried forward:

1. **Can the PSF relabel cost be cut by 2–10× without dropping return?** — motivating a size ablation over {12.7M, 8M, 4M, 2M, 1M} rows, ranked by `return_to_go[ep_start]`.
2. **Does embedding *only the Prediction: suffix* of the Gemini response produce a policy that reads the forecast more than the full-response embedding?** — Track A, Prediction-only embeddings at both train and eval.

## Experiment 31 — PSF size ablation (AWR pretrain only)

Five AWR pretrain runs on the cadence=5 PSF gemini_emb lineage, varying training rows only. Top-K subsets built by `pipeline/build_psf_size_subsets.py`: rank episodes by `return_to_go[ep_start]` desc, walk until cumulative row target hit, repack with `text_generated` dropped but `hidden_state` fp16 preserved.

Recipe held constant across all five: β=10, ofrac=0.05, ow=0.0, 100K steps, lr=3e-4, hidden-mode=real, layer-width=512, hidden-dim=3072. Only `--data-dir` varies.

### Result

| Subset | Rows | 50-ep return (mean ± std) | val acc_real | Δ(zero−real) | Δ(shuf−real) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| full | 12.7M | 17.48 ± 4.32 | 42.52% | +0.033 | +0.015 |
| top8M | 8M | 17.00 ± 4.57 | 41.59% | +0.061 | +0.029 |
| top4M | 4M | **17.72 ± 3.93** | 39.82% | +0.046 | +0.034 |
| top2M | 2M | 16.86 ± 4.77 | 38.93% | +0.089 | +0.060 |
| top1M | 1M | 15.12 ± 5.23 | 35.12% | +0.145 | +0.135 |

SE on each mean ≈ 0.55–0.74 (std/√50). Pairwise differences within {full, top8M, top4M, top2M} are all within 1σ combined; top1M is ~2σ below the band.

### Interpretation

- **Return stays flat from 12.7M down to 2M rows** — roughly 6× data reduction at no measurable cost to this AWR-only checkpoint. top4M is nominally the best of the five (17.72) but within noise of full. Knee is between 2M and 1M.
- **top4M's lower variance** (σ=3.93 vs 4.32–5.23 elsewhere) is suggestive but a single run per point; don't over-read.
- **Hidden-branch sensitivity grows monotonically as data shrinks** (Δ(zero−real): 0.033 → 0.061 → 0.046 → 0.089 → 0.145). The smaller-data policies lean more on the hidden pathway to get their logits up. This is a mechanism probe, not a return probe: it tells us the policy is willing to use the branch when the obs branch is undertrained, which is consistent with all prior observations that imagination reading is strongly gated by training signal sufficiency on obs.
- **Val acc_real drops steadily** (42.5% → 35.1%) in line with expected undertraining of the obs branch at smaller datasets. This is the dominant driver of the top1M return drop; the 4.5% accuracy shortfall matches the ~2.4 return drop.

### What this does not close

- These are AWR-only pretrain checkpoints, not freezenone fine-tunes. The Apr-19 result of interest (18.58 freezenone) came from AWR + freezenone. The size ablation was run AWR-only to keep the comparison apples-to-apples between subsets (adding freezenone adds recipe complexity); but it means the numbers here are ~1–2 pp below the usable freezenone operating point. A single freezenone fine-tune on top2M or top4M would confirm whether the "6× data reduction is free" conclusion survives into the production recipe.
- The top4M edge over full (17.72 vs 17.48) is ~0.5σ; plausibly a quality filter effect (dropping the lowest-return episodes denoises the gradient) but single-run.

### Immediate practical implication

Future PSF relabels (e.g., for new prompt variants) can be done on top2M or top4M episodes rather than the full 158-file set, cutting Gemini API cost ~3–6×. `pipeline/build_psf_size_subsets.py` was added to produce these from any source lineage.

## Experiment 32 — Track A: Prediction-only embeddings (in flight)

Hypothesis: training embeddings of only the `Prediction: ...` suffix (concise, forecast-bearing) rather than the full response (State Understanding + Prediction) gives the policy a cleaner signal and may surface content-usage the full-response embeddings diffuse. Matching inference-time embeddings via a new `--extract-prediction-only` flag on `eval/eval_online.py`.

### Early returns (in-flight, 5/50 episodes)

23.10, 20.10, 18.10, 18.10, 19.10 → running mean **19.7** after 5 episodes. Comfortably above the full-response freezenone baseline (18.82 ± 3.89) and the unaug ceiling (18.38). Too early to call — another 45 episodes to land.

### Validation split (held-out)

- AWR pretrain val_acc_real = 0.3993 (vs full-response pretrain 0.4252); lower absolute accuracy, but on a different data distribution.
- Freezenone val (oracle-full data, end-of-training): acc_real = 0.9068, Δ(real−zero) = +0.807. Caution: this is oracle-full data, so the giant Δ reads like memorization through the hidden branch. On held-out PSF predonly shards (`eval.validate_awr --file-offset 117 --max-files 8`): Δ NLL(zero−real) = +0.194, acc delta = −2.67pp. Closer to the "mild content-sensitivity" regime from the full-response run.

### What this run tests

Whether the policy's response to imagination content shifts when both training and inference embeddings are restricted to the Prediction slice. Expected failure mode: if the information the policy was reading in the full-response embeddings lived in the State Understanding section (description of *now*, not the forecast), predonly will drop. Expected success mode: if the policy is currently content-blind because the Prediction signal is drowned in State Understanding tokens, predonly lifts it.

## Fidelity vs. policy-side bottleneck — open discussion

After Apr-19 closed with "content-invariance holds under fresh-horizon alignment", two mechanism hypotheses remain live:

1. **Fidelity hypothesis.** The predictions Gemini emits are too often wrong — the policy learns to ignore them. Under this hypothesis, better predictions (thinking prompt, or oracle-grade predictions formatted as forecasts) should raise content-sensitivity and push return above unaug 18.38.
2. **Policy-side hypothesis.** The predictions are fine; the policy architecture cannot localize the forecast-relevant dimensions of the embedding, or imagination is genuinely redundant with obs for Craftax-tier dynamics. Under this hypothesis, even upper-bound fidelity (grounded oracle predictions) will plateau at ~18.

Planned discriminating experiment: top2M pipeline rerun with **grounded-as-if-predicting** prompt (Gemini sees the next-5-step rollout but is instructed to output only a forward-looking "Prediction: ..." section, phrased as a forecast). If this matches or meaningfully beats unaug, fidelity is contributing; if it plateaus, the limiter is downstream. Paired soft intervention: top2M with **thinking** prompt (Gemini allocated a thinking budget before emitting Prediction).
