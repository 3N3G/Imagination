### Interpretation

- **Same recipe, fresh labels, +2.62 return.** Holding β, ofrac, ow, freeze, lr, arch, and pretrained-init schedule constant, the only changes between Apr-17 gemini_emb freezenone and v2 are: (i) `obs_to_text` now emits equipment fields, (ii) gameplay.py survivefix + stations fixes, (iii) labeller upgraded from 2.5-flash to 3.1-flash. The lift is larger than all previous gemini_emb recipe sweeps combined (Apr-17 Exp 19's 8 configs had a 4.4pp spread and only one config above baseline).
- **First parity with unaug 18.38 for any augmented policy.** Previously every augmented policy was ≥0.5pp below unaug; v2 is +0.44 above (within 1σ but the *direction* has flipped for the first time since model-size sweep).
- **Length 702 ± 516** is long — the policy is surviving, not just speed-running. Achievement count 19/28 ≈ matches qwen3gen pattern.
- The Apr-17 Exp 20 HP/food probe showed gemini_emb had the cleanest correct-sign content-to-V mapping even pre-fix; the v2 lift is consistent with that signal finally landing on a policy that can act on it, now that the imagination text describes the player's actual equipment.

### What this does not close

- **We have not re-run the content probes on the v2 checkpoint.** The +2.62 lift could come from (a) richer imagination content unlocking better hidden-pathway usage, or (b) the stations+survivefix prompts producing narratives that are just obs-independent strategy nuggets the policy latches onto through the obs branch. HP/food perturbation + die/adversarial prompt probes on v2 would localize this.
- **Only the freezenone recipe was re-tried.** β, ofrac, ow, freeze sweeps from Apr-17 have not been repeated on the fixed pipeline — the v2 surface could look very different (e.g., the encoder-specific preferences for β=30 vs β=3 may shift).
- **qwen3gen and qwen3emb have not been re-run on fixed labels.** Apr-17 ranked qwen3emb > qwen3gen > gemini_emb by return (17.88 / 17.58 / 16.20). v2 flipped gemini_emb to top; whether the other two also benefit is open.
- **50 eps, std 3.89** — a 100-ep rerun would tighten the comparison to unaug noticeably (SE 0.55 → 0.39).

## Known pitfalls recorded

- **AWR pretrain needs 256G mem.** 128G OOM'd at ~500 steps on this dataset. Dataset is ~159 GiB unsharded → 60 GB sharded per shard; peak with model, stats, and validation exceeds 128G. Updated the default in `v2_awr_pretrain_gemini_emb_run.sh` assumption.
- **wandb service fails to start on login1.** Retroactive sync jobs must be submitted via SLURM to a compute node (the login-node failure mode is a silent port-file timeout after 30s).

## Follow-on experiments (planned, this session)

1. **Content probes on v2 freezenone** — Replay Exp 16 (die/adversarial prompts) and Exp 20 (HP/food perturbation) to verify the +2.62 lift is driven by content-reading, not content-blindness with obs improvements.
2. **β sweep on v2** — Apr-17 Exp 19 β=30 was optimal for pre-fix gemini_emb; refit on v2 data to confirm. β ∈ {10, 30, 50, 100}.
3. **Encoder bake-off on v2** — Redo qwen3gen, qwen3emb on v2 data with matching freezenone recipe. Pre-fix rank was qwen3gen > qwen3emb > gemini_emb; v2 may reshuffle.
4. **Longer training** — Apr-17 100K runs hurt pre-fix; re-examine now that validation memorization may correspond to *correct* content retention.

Primary candidate to launch first: the v2 content probes, since they validate the interpretation before committing GPU to further sweeps.
