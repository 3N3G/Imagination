## Model Size Experiment (Mar 28)

6 models: aug/unaug × w512/w1024/w2048, all 100K steps.

| Model | Params | Return | Std |
|-------|--------|--------|-----|
| aug-w512 | 14.3M | 16.20 | 5.11 |
| **unaug-w512** | 9.5M | **17.70** | 2.69 |
| aug-w1024 | 31.7M | 14.50 | 4.72 |
| **unaug-w1024** | 21.2M | **19.10** | 1.90 |
| **aug-w2048** | 75.9M | **18.90** | 2.14 |
| unaug-w2048 | 50.7M | 17.40 | 3.80 |

**Finding:** Augmentation only helps at w2048. Below that, unaugmented wins. Best: unaug-w1024=19.10.
