### Key v5 findings:
1. **LR=1e-4 dominates** — best Δ=+0.47 vs +0.41 at 3e-4 vs +0.24 at 1e-3
2. **LayerNorm is critical** — all no-LN configs have Δ < 0.19 (vs 0.37+ with LN)
3. **Oracle weight insensitive** in [0.5, 2.0] range — all ≈ +0.42-0.47
4. **Beta insensitive** in [1, 10] — all ≈ +0.39-0.47
5. **Width doesn't help** — w1024 slightly worse than w512 at same LR
6. **Controls work** — ow=0 gives Δ≈0, confirming metric validity
7. **real > shuf > zero** ordering achieved for first time (PSF training data fixed this)
